Nigerian Constitution App

 Nigerian Constitution is a collection of constitution of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, which commenced on 5th May 1999 according to Decree No. 24. This App is to help citizens of Nigeria learn more about the constitution. The App Contains all sections and parts (sub-sections) of the constitution.

Nigerian Criminal Code Act

 Nigerian Criminal Code Act is a collection of nigerian criminal offences and their respective laws to help citizens of Nigeria learn more about the Nigerian criminal law. The App Contains all Parts, Divisions and Chapters of the criminal code act.

Jamb CBT

 Jamb CBT is a collection of thousands of Solved past questions on Jamb (Joint Admission and Matriculation Board) Examination to help Nigerian students in preparation for the Jamb Examination. This App presently contains Subjects such as English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Economics, Government, Literature, CRK and Geography. Download this app and start preparing for your Jamb Examination anywhere you go.
